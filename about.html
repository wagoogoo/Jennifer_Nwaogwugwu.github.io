Who am I?

I'm a User Experience Designer in ATX with a passion for usability. I believe that Effectiveness, Efficiency, and Satisfaction all contribute to making an enjoyable interaction between people and their systems.

Before my Master degree, I received my undergraduate degree in Communication Science and Disorders at the University of Texas at Austin. May of 2019 I'll receive my degree in Master of Science in Information Studies with a focus in User Experience Design and User Research, also from the University of Texas at Austin. In my free time you can find me playing my guitar, reading Lord of the Rings, or watching movies at Alamo Drafthouse with my friends.
